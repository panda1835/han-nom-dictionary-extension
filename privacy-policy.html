Privacy Policy for Hán-Nôm Dictionary Extension

Effective Date: November 3, 2025

Summary: The Hán-Nôm Dictionary extension does not collect, store, or share any personal information. It only stores your preference for enabling/disabling the extension locally on your device.

1. Introduction

This Privacy Policy explains how the Hán-Nôm Dictionary browser extension ("the Extension") handles user data. We are committed to protecting your privacy and being transparent about our data practices.

2. Information We Do NOT Collect

The Extension does NOT collect, transmit, or store:

  • Personal information (name, email, address, etc.)
  • Browsing history or web activity
  • The content of web pages you visit
  • Search queries or characters you look up
  • IP addresses or location data
  • Cookies or tracking identifiers
  • Any analytics or usage statistics

3. Information Stored Locally

The Extension stores only the following information locally on your device using Chrome's storage API:

  • Extension State: Whether the extension is enabled or disabled (on/off toggle)

This data:

  • Remains on your device only
  • Is never transmitted to any server
  • Can be cleared by removing the extension

4. Network Requests

When you click on a Hán-Nôm character, the Extension makes a network request to:

  • API Endpoint: https://digitizingvietnam.com or https://www.digitizingvietnam.com
  • Purpose: To fetch dictionary definitions for the character you clicked
  • Data Sent: Only the single character being looked up

These requests are made directly from your browser to the Digitizing Vietnam API. The API follows standard web server practices and may log requests for operational purposes (such as preventing abuse), but this data is not used for tracking or identification.

5. Third-Party Services

The Extension communicates only with the Digitizing Vietnam API (digitizingvietnam.com) to retrieve dictionary definitions. We do not use any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services.

6. Data Security

Since the Extension does not collect or store personal information, there is minimal security risk. The API requests are made over HTTPS to ensure secure communication.

7. Children's Privacy

The Extension does not collect any information from anyone, including children under the age of 13. It is safe for users of all ages.

8. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated effective date. Continued use of the Extension after changes constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.

9. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Disable or uninstall the Extension at any time
  • Clear locally stored preferences by removing the Extension
  • Contact us with questions about this Privacy Policy

10. Open Source

The Extension's source code is available for review, demonstrating our commitment to transparency. You can verify that the Extension operates exactly as described in this Privacy Policy.

If you have any questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy or the Extension's data practices, please contact us:
